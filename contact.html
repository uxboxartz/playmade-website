Frequently Asked Questions.

Active and Healthy Communities UI +

PlayPods helps foster a healthy, active lifestyle by making sports equipment accessible to everyone. We're dedicated to helping people stay active in their communities.

How can I participate? +

You can participate by signing up on our website, finding a PlayPod location near you, and borrowing equipment for free to enjoy an active lifestyle.

Is PlayPods available in my city? +

We are continuously expanding to new cities! Check our website for available locations, or reach out to request PlayPods in your area.

How do I return the equipment? +

Simply return the equipment to the designated PlayPod station where you borrowed it. Each station is equipped with clear instructions on how to return items.

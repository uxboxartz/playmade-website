Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2025

Welcome to PlayMade, operated by Cosist LTD ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use PlayMade.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and account details.
  • Location Data: If you enable location services, we collect your location to help you discover nearby sports areas and events.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app, including game participation, messages, and preferences.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve PlayMade’s features and services.
  • Enable communication between users.
  • Process transactions and manage shared sports equipment access.
  • Ensure security, prevent fraud, and enforce our terms.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it with:

  • Service providers assisting with app functionality (e.g., hosting, payments, analytics).
  • Other users, when necessary for app functionality (e.g., chat and game invitations).
  • Legal authorities, if required to comply with laws or protect user safety.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. However, no online platform is 100% secure, so use PlayMade responsibly.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data via your account settings.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request data deletion by contacting us at go@playmade.co.uk.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, track app performance, and analyze user interactions.

7. Third-Party Links

PlayMade may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, so review their policies separately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of PlayMade after updates means you accept the changes.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: go@playmade.co.uk
